  • How to Sign out from the app?
    Go to home page
    >click on ‘settings’ on top right side
    >select ‘signout'
  • How to reset your password?
    In the login page click on the forgot password option….
    >enter the registered Athrv-ed mail-id
    >click on ‘submit’
    >check your mail inbox and open a mail from athrvservices
    >click on the link provided which will redirect you to the new reset password page.
    >set your new password here,and click on ‘submit’
    > your password is successfully updated, try login again with new password.
    >time limit for reset password is 10mins from you getting the mail
  • How to create your group?
    Go to home page
    >click on ‘+’ on left top
    >give the group name
    >select the members->upload a group pic
    Tada!! Your new group created successfully.
  • How to edit a group profile?
    Goto the group that you want to edit->click on group title at top-> edit group page will appear->members list will appear
    1. You can change the group name by simply typing new group name
    2. Click on the group pic if you want to upload a new group image.
    3. Click on ‘update’ to successfully edit your group after all changes.
    4. Click on ‘delete group’ if you want to delete that particular group permanently.
    Yo!!!! Group is updated
  • How to manage members in group?
    Goto the group that you want to edit->click on group title at top-> edit group page will appear->
    1. select ‘add participants’ to add new members to the group.
    2. Select ‘remove’ to remove an existing member.
    3. Select ‘admin’ to make an existing member admin of the group.
    4. Click on ‘update’ to successfully edit your group after all changes.
    Done! done! done!
  • Can we have a personal chat using a chatapp?
    Current version of chat app is designed only for group chats, however you can create a group of 2 people(yourself and other person) and chat if required.
  • Not able to register for a chat app?
    Check your registered mailID and userID, both should match in order to have successful registration
  • How to know if a member is online or not?
    Always when a group is opened, below the chat title you will get the count of members online and total members in that particular group.
    For a person to be online two conditions required:
    1.Authenticated user login.
    2.Chat app browser tab should be open.
    In the participant list the green dot in front of username represents that member is online
  • How to check if I'm an admin or not?
    Go to the participant list, if there is an ‘Admin’ label in front of your name, then you are the admin of the group.
